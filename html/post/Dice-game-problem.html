Dice game

Suppose we play a game. I roll a die up to three times. Each time I roll, you can either take the number showing as dollars, or roll again.
What are your expected winnings?

  • You roll a die up to three times.
  • After each roll, you can either take the result in dollars or roll again.
  • If you roll a third time, you must accept the third roll.
    • The goal is to maximize your expected winnings by choosing when to stop rolling.
